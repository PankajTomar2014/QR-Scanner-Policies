Help and Support for QR Scanner App

If you have any questions or need assistance with the QR Scanner App, you’ve come to the right place. Below are some common issues and solutions, as well as how to contact us for further support.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I use the QR Scanner App?

To use the QR Scanner App, simply open the app and point your device’s camera at the QR code you wish to scan. The app will automatically detect and process the QR code.

2. Why is the camera not working?

If the camera is not working, please check the following:

  • Ensure that you have granted the app permission to access your camera.
  • Restart the app and try again.
  • Restart your device if the issue persists.

3. How can I provide feedback or report a problem?

You can provide feedback or report problems by contacting us directly. See the "Contact Us" section below for details.

4. The app is not scanning QR codes properly. What should I do?

If the app is not scanning QR codes properly:

  • Make sure the QR code is well-lit and in focus.
  • Ensure there are no obstructions or glare on the QR code.
  • Try cleaning your device’s camera lens.

5. How do I save or share the QR code I generated?

After generating a QR code, you can usually save or share it directly from the app. Look for options like "Save" or "Share" within the app's interface to export or send the QR code.

6. Can I use the app offline?

The QR Scanner App requires an internet connection to download and update data. However, scanning QR codes and generating new ones can typically be done offline.

7. Is my data secure with this app?

Your privacy and data security are important to us. The app does not store or transmit personal data, and all operations are performed locally on your device. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
