Privacy Policy for QR Scanner App

Effective Date: 20-September-2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our QR Scanner app.

Information We Collect

  • Camera Access: To scan QR codes. We do not store or collect any data from the camera.
  • Gallery Access: To detect QR codes from images in your gallery. No images or data are stored or collected.
  • Generated QR Codes: Created QR codes are processed locally on your device. We do not collect or store this data.

In-App Browser

Our in-app browser allows you to open web links directly within the app. We do not track or store your browsing activity.

Feedback and Reviews

We may collect your feedback or reviews to improve our services. No personal data is collected through the feedback form.

Data Storage and Security

All data generated or used by the app is stored locally on your device. We do not store or transmit any personal information to our servers or third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page.
